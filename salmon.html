Salmon


Description

This recipe is for a perfectly cooked salmon that can be done in less than 20 minutes.

With only a few, simple ingredients you can make this mouthwatering salmon yourself at home.

Ingredients

  • 5 oz Skinless Salmon
  • Olive oil
  • Zucchini
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • White rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Old bay seafood seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Measure out 1 cup of uncooked white rice and add it to a rice cooker. Start the rice cooker.
  3. Cut up Zucchini into preferred size
  4. Season the salmon with the Old bay seasoning to your liking.
  5. Place the salmon on a baking sheet and place in the oven. Set a timer for 15 minutes.
  6. While the salmon and rice are cooking heat a pan on medium heat.
  7. Add olive oil to the pan and cook Zucchini until golden.
  8. When timer is complete for the salmon, turn on the broiler and cook an additional 2 minutes on the Hi broiler setting.
  9. Add cooked salmon, zucchini, and rice on a plate. Add soy sauce to rice to taste and enjoy!
